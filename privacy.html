Privacy Policy

Last updated September 14, 2018

Our policy is short and sweet because we value your information & privacy. We don't track or collect any personally identifiable information from our users.   • Ad supported apps contain Google's Mobile Ads SDK with AdMob enabled. Please refer to their policies.
