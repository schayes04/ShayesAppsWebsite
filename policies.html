Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Privacy Policy

Last updated December 23, 2020

Our policy is short and sweet because we value your information & privacy. We don't track or collect any personally identifiable information from our users. Any information collected is by 3rd party services and we have no access or visibility to it. We do our best to limit the number of 3rd parties we rely on.

  • Payments for in-app purchases & subscriptions are done through Apple's App Store, so we never have access to any of your credit card information. Subscription apps contain the RevenueCat SDK. Please refer to their policies.
  • Ad supported apps contain Google's Mobile Ads SDK with AdMob enabled. Please refer to their policies.
  • iCloud data synchronization is done using Apple's CloudKit. CloudKit is fully encrypted and all data lives on Apple's infrastructure. No one besides you has access to your data.

    • If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us: support@shayesapps.com
