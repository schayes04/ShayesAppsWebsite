    • iCloud Sync

    If data is not syncing seemlessly across your devices, please follow the steps below on each of your devices.

    1. Verify iCloud settings
    iOS
  • Open iOS Settings app
  • Tap your name (near the top)
  • Verify the Apple ID (name & email) shown is the same across all your devices
  • Tap iCloud
  • Find our application in the "Saved to iCloud" list (formerly "Apps using iCloud") list & verify it is enabled
  • For Countdowns only: verify iCloud Drive is enabled & Countdowns is enabled in "Apps Syncing to iCloud Drive".

    • macOS
  • Open Apple menu ()
  • Choose System Settings
  • Click your name (near the top)
  • Verify the Apple ID (name & email) shown is the same across all your devices
  • Tap iCloud
  • Click "See All" under "Saved to iCloud" (formerly "Show More Apps" under "Apps Using iCloud")
  • Find our application in the applications list & verify it is enabled
  • For Countdowns only: verify iCloud Drive is enabled & Countdowns is enabled in "Apps Syncing to iCloud Drive".

    • 2. Check iCloud storage space

    Syncing requires that you have enough available iCloud storage space.

    3. Update your software

    iCloud Sync will work best with the latest softwate updates. Please make sure all your devices have the latest version of our application installed. Also make sure you are using the latest versions of iOS, iPadOS, and macOS.

    4. Restart your devices

    Apple Watch Sync

    If data is not syncing to your Apple Watch, please follow the steps below.

    1. Restart your devices

    Restarting both your iPhone & Apple Watch is typically able to resolve any sync issues.

    2. Update your software

    Apple Watch sync will work best with the latest softwate updates. Please make sure all your devices have the latest version of our application installed. Also make sure you are using the latest versions of iOS and watchOS.
