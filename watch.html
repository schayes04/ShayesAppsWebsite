Complications Not Showing

Due to changes in Countdowns version 8, some Apple Watch Complications will stop displaying properly. You may see -- or ---- being displayed on your Watch face instead. Please follow the steps to resolve this issue:

  1. Open the Watch app on your iPhone
  2. Navigate to the My Watch tab (if necessary)
  3. Choose the watch face with the missing complication
  4. Scroll down to the Complications section
  5. Tap to modify the desired complication position
  6. Locate Countdowns app and choose Next Upcoming or the specific countdown you'd like to see
